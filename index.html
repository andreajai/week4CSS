I would like to talk about two artist I recently discovered. Their names are CA7RIEL & Paco Amoroso. I came across their Tiny Desk concert they did for Latino Heritage Month. I truly was blown away by this performance! They're so many different sounds, genres combined to perfect this beautiful orchestra. I can’t even describe what I heard. But it remined me a lot of a place I LOVE TO GO TO. There is this tiny, hole in the wall place in Bushwick that I love to go with my partner after a night out. We like to the end the night here. When we go there our motive is to DANCE. The DJ is sitting on a higher step at the right corner side of the room parallel to the bar. There is one single red light that is lit above the DJ booth. The whole time the crowd is DANCING. This is not a place where you stand on the side. The music is always bumping. Sometimes, I think its just a bunch of sounds.

BOOP BI BOOP BA BOOP

I usually like to close my eyes and dance the whole time. My head is quiet during this time, and I am focused on the sounds. When I saw the CA7RIEL & Paco Amoroso tiny desk concert I thought to myself WOW. This is what I love to listen to. I am so glad I came across it.


I would like to describe their sound as a compilation of jazz, Latin trap, and pure chaos. Their song La Que Puede, Puede reminds me of the theme song of Cowboy Bebop. SO GOOD.
